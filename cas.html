CAS

What is CAS?

CAS, stands for Creativity, Activity and Service and is one of the three essential elements that every student must complete. CAS helps students to improve their personal and interpersonal growth by experiential learning. All students of the IB are expected to complete a CAS curriculum that must be recorded at least about 18 months during the 2 years of the IB Diploma. The three parts are characterized as the following:

    Creativity: arts, and other experiences that involve creative thinking.
    Activity: physical exertion contributing to a healthy lifestyle.
    Service: an unpaid and voluntary exchange that has a learning benefit for the student.
